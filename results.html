What 10x ROAS Means

Real money.

10x ROAS means for every $1 you spend on ads, you get $10 back in revenue. On a $3,000 wrap job that started with a $300 ad lead, that's the math.

But wrap shops scale faster than most businesses because one fleet account can be worth $50,000–$200,000 per year. A single commercial client acquired through ads doesn't just mean one job — it means a relationship.
