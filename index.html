Customer acquisition systems for vehicle wrap shops and vehicle service businesses

ZScaledIt is a media buying and customer acquisition agency built specifically for vehicle service businesses: commercial and retail vehicle wrap shops, paint protection film (PPF) installers, ceramic coating shops, window tint businesses, auto glass companies, and detailing operations. The agency was founded on direct experience inside the vehicle wrap industry, where it built a system now delivering an average of 10 to 12x return on ad spend for active clients.

About this page

What ZScaledIt does differently

Generalist marketing agencies apply the same playbook to every local business, regardless of what that business actually sells or how its buyers make decisions. ZScaledIt builds a complete acquisition system shaped around how vehicle service businesses sell: the difference between a commercial fleet wrap account and a retail color change job, the buyer psychology behind a PPF purchase versus a ceramic coating add-on, and the creative angles that make a vehicle transformation stop someone mid-scroll. Read more about the five-step method ZScaledIt runs for every new client.

Services included in every engagement

Every ZScaledIt client receives Meta Ads management, Google Ads management, CRM setup with automated SMS and email follow-up, a custom landing page built for their city and service mix, Meta Pixel and Conversions API tracking, Google Tag conversion tracking, 15 or more monthly ad creative variations, weekly performance reporting, and ongoing sales process consulting. See the full breakdown on the method page or review live client results.

The market exclusivity model

ZScaledIt caps its roster at 20 clients and takes only one business per local market, regardless of vehicle service niche. This means a commercial wrap shop and a detailing business in the same city cannot both be ZScaledIt clients, protecting each client's competitive position. Learn more about current market availability on the contact page.

Industries served

ZScaledIt works with vehicle wrap shops (commercial fleet and retail color change), paint protection film (PPF) installers, ceramic coating applicators, window tint businesses, auto glass repair and replacement companies, and vehicle detailing operations across the United States. Active clients are currently located in Florida, Minnesota, Louisiana, Tennessee, Illinois, and Texas, with most other US markets still open. See the about page for founder background and the reasoning behind the niche-first approach.

The $500 ad spend offer

New ZScaledIt clients receive their first $500 in ad spend covered by the agency, so the first campaign runs with real money behind it and no upfront ad budget risk on the client's side. This mirrors the offer structure used by other performance-focused growth partners in the small business marketing space.
