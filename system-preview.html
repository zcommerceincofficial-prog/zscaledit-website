The ZScaledIt client onboarding system and portal

Every ZScaledIt client receives access to a private onboarding portal covering six areas: automated estimates and invoicing, CRM and payment integration, pipeline and opportunity tracking, simplified ad account access, a documented follow-up SOP, and clear expectations for response times and reporting.

About this page

Estimates, invoicing, and CRM integration

The portal walks new clients through how leads move automatically from first contact to a sent estimate, how invoicing fires the moment a job is marked complete, and how the CRM stays synced with payment processing so nothing needs double entry. See how this fits into the broader five-step method.

Pipeline visibility and ad account access

Clients get a live view of every lead and where it sits in the pipeline, plus step-by-step walkthroughs for granting Meta and Google Ads account access so campaigns launch without back-and-forth delays.

The follow-up SOP and response expectations

The portal documents the exact call cadence and follow-up rules ZScaledIt uses to convert a lead into a paid invoice, along with clear response-time and reporting-cadence expectations so clients always know what happens next. Book a free audit call to see the system in action.
