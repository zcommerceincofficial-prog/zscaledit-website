Common Questions

Before you book, quick answers.

Do I need to already be running ads?

No. We build everything from scratch if needed: ad accounts, landing pages, CRM, tracking. Starting from zero is fine. We have done it many times.

What markets are still available?

We have active clients in Jacksonville FL, Tampa FL, Apple Valley MN, New Orleans LA, Clarksville TN, Rock Island IL, Mahomet IL, and Texas. Most other US cities are still open. Book the call and we will confirm your city on the spot.

What's the monthly retainer?

Pricing is discussed on the audit call after we understand your market, your services, and your goals. We do not publish a flat rate because the right investment varies by market size and services offered.

How long until I see results?

Ads are live by day 8. Most clients see their first qualified leads in week one. The real data, what is working and what to scale, comes in weeks 2 to 4. We do not make 30-day guarantees because we would rather give you honest timelines than incentivize us to chase vanity numbers.

Do you work with shops outside the US?

Currently US-only. We may expand to Canada in 2027. Reach out if you are in a Canadian market and want to be first on the list.
